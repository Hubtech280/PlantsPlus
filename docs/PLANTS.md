# Plant mechanics

## Lotus Pumpkin — 6000

Pumpkin + Snow Lotus. A defensive fusion with a five-charge heal for the protected plant.

## Bambnut — 6001

Bamblock + Wall-nut. A defensive Snow/Day cross fusion.

## Magnet-o-pea — 6002

Peashooter + Magnet-shroom. Absorbs one supported metal item and changes its projectile mode. Supported modes include bucket, football helmet, jack-in-the-box, chrono/portal heart and Giga Mecha Fragments.

## Iceberg-shroom — 6003

Ice-shroom + Ice-shroom. Deals 40 damage and freezes for 8 seconds. Freeze-immune enemies are slowed instead of frozen.

## Witchfire Pumpkin — 6004

Pyro Pumpkin + Doom Pumpkin. Weak Odyssey plant with 4000 toughness. It buffs and heals the protected plant while unlit, then sacrifices protected plants and triggers Doom/Jalapeno effects while lit. It supports the Grenades and Radiation Odyssey modifiers.

## Nutty Sharpshooter — 6005

Spruce Sharpshooter + Wall-nut. Has 4000 toughness and fires a piercing, knockback projectile that damages handheld armor normally.

## Inferno Torchflower — 6006

Advanced Alt of Infernowood. Infernowood + Sunflower creates it; Torchwood converts it back. It stores Sun from eligible projectiles and releases the reserve when fully energized.

## Pumpkin Podbomber — 6007

Advanced Alt of Explode-o-shooter. Explode-o-shooter + Pumpkin creates it. It copies a protected Pea-family plant and periodically replaces volleys with Explode-o-peas. The Shovel returns an Explode-o-shooter seed packet.

## Ceasarweed — 6008

Advanced Alt based on Spikeweed and Melon-pult. Salad-pult + Spikeweed creates it; Melon-pult converts it back to Salad-pult. It fires 1 + X salads, up to 10, based on missed lobbed projectiles. Salads butter targets for 4 seconds. Normal zombies and Zombonis ignore it, while Gargantuars can attack it.

## Solar Firnace — 6009

Firnace absorbs the Sunflower directly below it. Solar Firnace is a flying plant with 4000 toughness, a 90-second lifetime and 25 Sun production every 25 seconds. Clicking spends 100 Sun to add 45 seconds; each later extension costs 50 more Sun. It returns to Firnace at zero.
